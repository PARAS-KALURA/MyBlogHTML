My Favourite Hobby: Over-Thinking

SUMMARY: Overthinking refers to the act of dwelling excessively on thoughts, often leading to anxiety, confusion, or indecision. It involves analyzing situations or problems in great detail, often to the point where it becomes unproductive and stressful.


MANAGE:

  • Physical movement: Walking can help release tension and reduce stress, allowing your mind to relax.

  • Change of environment:A new setting, especially outdoors, can help break the cycle of rumination and give you a fresh perspective.

  • Mindfulness: Walking lets you focus on the present moment—your surroundings, the rhythm of your steps—helping to redirect your thoughts.

  • Endorphins:Physical activity triggers the release of endorphins, which are natural mood boosters, reducing anxiety and improving overall well-being.
